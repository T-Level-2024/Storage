The microwave oven:


The microwave oven is a common household device used for heating various things, although commonly used for heating food. It is a powered appliance, hence it requires electricity to operate.

The microwave oven is named after the usage of microwave radiation in the appliance, which is the primary source of heat. It is popular due to it's ability to quickly heat things without them experiencing the melting that would occur with pan-based cooking.

It should be noted that microwave radiation is harmful to humans. It can cause severe burn wounds, and inflict deep wounds to tissue. There also appears to be several accidents where people have received burns while using the microwave, so caution should be exercised during use.

Due to the ability that the microwave oven can provide rapid heating, some food and packaging are designed to use this effect. A notable product that uses this is microwave popcorn, which allows popcorn to expand.
